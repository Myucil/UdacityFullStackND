Front-end Kuroko


Kuroko moves in the background, almost but not quite invisible, pulling all the strings, making things real, believable and useable.
I have been a Kuroko on stage and in my day-job as a physical actor and dance-performer I weave my body in and out of situations to create meaning through symbolic gesture.

In much the same way, a programmer creates meaning using the symbolism of code, making apps, sites and insights appear seemingly out of nothing, through insistant and insessant pushing of keys.

Full Stack Nanodegree

I am currently going through the Full Stack Nanodegree. I am planning to use what I learn here to create a web-app and database for Danish performing artists. This app/database will be used to share technical equipment, theatre lights, costumes etc. so that we can all use our limited funds for creating art rather than for buying and renting equipment.
